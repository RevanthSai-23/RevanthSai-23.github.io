CASE_03

One Power App, every brand — the automotive platform for Al-Futtaim's showrooms

Al-Futtaim Automotive · Lexus / Toyota / BYD / Honda / Jeep / Denza & more

Problem

Al-Futtaim runs showrooms for many automotive brands across the UAE, each with its own teams and processes. Sales Executives and Guest Relations Officers were working in siloed, brand-by-brand tools — no single place to create leads, appointments, and test drives, and no clean link to the systems of record.

Approach

A single centralized Power Apps platform serving every brand. On login, the app reads the user's profile from SAP C4C and resolves their brand at runtime — automatically rendering the right brand experience for that SE or GRO. Leads, appointments, and test drives are created and managed end-to-end in-app, driven by dozens of Power Automate flows wired into microservices and SAP C4C. An Azure AI Foundry agent (custom GPT-4o-mini) sits on top for natural-language schedule queries. Shipped across Dev, UAT, and Production.

CASE_04

End-to-end real estate app — leads to bookings, synced with Yardi

Al-Futtaim · Real Estate Division

Problem

Real estate agents needed to run the full sales cycle — capturing leads, working opportunities, and confirming bookings — without bouncing between disconnected tools, and with everything reflected in Yardi, the property system of record.

Approach

A Power Apps application covering the entire lifecycle — leads → opportunities → viewings → bookings — handled in one interface and synced bidirectionally with the Yardi database through Power Automate. Added voice-to-action via AI Builder so agents create property viewings, leads, and appointments by speaking, cutting manual data entry across a high-volume pipeline.

Agents no longer stitch a lead in one system to a booking in another — the whole journey lives in a single Power App, and Yardi stays the source of truth automatically. Fewer handoffs, fewer dropped leads.
